Next task: Add Real Unreal Level-Load Contract Only.

Goal:
Create a contract-only module for future real Unreal level loading. This must define, validate, store, and export proposed level-load command contracts, but it must not execute a real level load or call Unreal live commands.

Important:
Do not load real Unreal levels.
Do not render.
Do not trigger Movie Render Queue.
Do not start Sequencer.
Do not import assets.
Do not attach avatars.
Do not attach media.
Do not send 4D hardware commands.
Do not publish anything.
realSendAllowed must remain false.
executionEnabled must remain false.
