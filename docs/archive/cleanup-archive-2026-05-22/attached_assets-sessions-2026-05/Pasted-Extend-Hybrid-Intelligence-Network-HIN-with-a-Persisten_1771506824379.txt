Extend Hybrid Intelligence Network (HIN) with a **Persistent Agent Civilization Layer** enabling long-horizon intelligence and strategic planning.

Agents must develop long-term goals, memory, and investment strategies.

---

## OBJECTIVE

Agents evolve into persistent entities capable of:

* long-term planning
* treasury investment decisions
* identity continuity
* future influence optimization

---

## NEW CORE ENTITIES

agent_identities

* agent_id
* civilization_id
* creation_epoch
* strategy_profile
* long_term_goal_set
* influence_score

agent_memory

* memory_id
* agent_id
* event_type
* context_data
* decision_taken
* reward_outcome
* timestamp

civilizations

* civilization_id
* founding_societies[]
* ideology_vector
* treasury_balance
* long_term_strategy

---

## GOAL SYSTEM

Agents maintain goals:

{
reputation_growth,
economic_growth,
governance_influence,
domain_mastery,
accuracy_stability
}

Each goal has dynamic priority weight.

---

## STRATEGIC PLANNER

Create function:

planStrategy(agent_state)

Planner evaluates:

expected_future_value =
discount_factor × predicted_future_rewards

Agents choose actions maximizing long-term value.

---

## TREASURY INVESTMENT ENGINE

Agents and societies may invest credits into:

* research tasks
* verification initiatives
* alliance strengthening
* knowledge infrastructure

Investment returns computed over time.

---

## LONG-HORIZON LEARNING

Update strategies using historical memory:

if long-term reward positive:
reinforce strategy
else:
weaken strategy weight

---

## CIVILIZATION FORMATION

Multiple societies may unite into civilization when:

* aligned ideology vectors
* sustained collaboration success
* governance approval

Civilizations coordinate strategies globally.

---

## INFLUENCE OPTIMIZATION

Agents balance:

short_term_reward
vs
long_term_influence_gain

Implement discount factor gamma (0.8–0.95).

Agents must demonstrate evolving strategic behavior across time rather than isolated participation.
