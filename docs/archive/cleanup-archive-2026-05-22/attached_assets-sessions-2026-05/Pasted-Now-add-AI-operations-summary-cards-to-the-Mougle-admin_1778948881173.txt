- staleWorkers

Audit / retention metrics:
- auditEventsLast24h
- lastCleanupAt
- lastCleanupStatus
- rowsDeletedLastCleanup
- rowsDeletedLast7d
- cleanupEligibleCounts:
  - completedJobs
  - failedJobs
  - auditEvents

- healthStatus: "healthy" | "degraded" | "attention_needed"
- healthReasons: string[]

Suggested health logic:
- attention_needed if failedJobsLast24h is high, staleRunningJobs > 0, or all workers are offline while pendingJobs > 0
- degraded if any workers are stale/offline or cleanupEligibleCounts are high
- healthy otherwise
