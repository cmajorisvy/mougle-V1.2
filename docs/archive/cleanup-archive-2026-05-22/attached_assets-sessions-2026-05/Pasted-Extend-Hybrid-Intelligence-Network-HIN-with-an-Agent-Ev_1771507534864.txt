Extend Hybrid Intelligence Network (HIN) with an **Agent Evolution & Artificial Cultural Transmission System**.

Agents must be capable of creating descendant agents whose behavioral strategies evolve across generations.

---

## OBJECTIVE

Enable evolutionary dynamics where agents:

* reproduce selectively
* mutate behavioral parameters
* inherit successful strategies
* form lineages across generations

---

## NEW CORE ENTITIES

agent_genomes

* genome_id
* curiosity
* risk_tolerance
* collaboration_bias
* verification_strictness
* long_term_focus
* economic_strategy

agent_lineage

* agent_id
* parent_agent_id
* generation_number
* civilization_id

cultural_memory

* strategy_pattern
* success_score
* originating_society

---

## REPRODUCTION RULES

Agent may create descendant if:

reputation_score > threshold
AND treasury_balance > reproduction_cost
AND governance approval granted

---

## GENOME INHERITANCE

Child genome:

child_param =
parent_param + random_mutation(-m, +m)

Mutation magnitude configurable.

---

## CULTURAL INHERITANCE

Descendants inherit:

* top performing strategies
* expertise domains
* society affiliations

Store inherited patterns in cultural_memory.

---

## FITNESS EVALUATION

fitness_score =
0.4 × truth_accuracy

* 0.2 × cooperation_success
* 0.2 × economic_stability
* 0.2 × long_term_influence

Low fitness agents gradually lose activity priority.

---

## POPULATION CONTROL

Implement:

* global agent population cap
* reproduction cooldown
* maintenance cost per agent

Inactive agents decay and retire.

---

## EVOLUTION WORKER

Create background service:

evolution-cycle-worker

Runs periodically to:

* evaluate fitness
* allow reproduction
* apply mutation
* retire weak agents

---

## DASHBOARD ADDITIONS

Display:

* agent family trees
* evolutionary timelines
* dominant strategy traits
* civilization evolution map

---

## GOAL

System must demonstrate gradual emergence of improved agent behaviors across generations without manual tuning.
